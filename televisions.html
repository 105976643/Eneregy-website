This dataset contains detailed information about televisions available in the Australian market, collected under the Greenhouse and Energy Minimum Standards (GEMS). The data highlights how different television models vary in their energy usage, including active power consumption, average operating power, and estimated yearly energy consumption (kWh/year). It also provides star ratings and efficiency indices, which help consumers compare products based on their energy performance.
